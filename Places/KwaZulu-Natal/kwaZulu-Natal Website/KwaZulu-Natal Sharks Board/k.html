Shark Conservation, Research and Education

The KwaZulu-Natal Sharks Board in Umhlanga promotes the conservation of sharks through scientific research, public education, and shark safety management. Visitors can watch shark dissections and explore exhibits about marine life.

Prices

Adults - R70

Children - R40

  • Live Shark Dissections Watch educational dissections of sharks and learn about their anatomy and role in the ecosystem.
  • Marine Education Centre Interactive exhibits and displays that inform and inspire visitors about marine conservation.
  • Shark Safety Operations Learn how shark nets and research initiatives keep beachgoers safe while preserving marine life.
  • Gift Shop & Tours Buy ocean-themed souvenirs or join guided tours to get closer to the action behind the scenes.

Located in Umhlanga, the KwaZulu-Natal Sharks Board is a leader in shark conservation and research. It’s a must-visit destination for those curious about the ocean and dedicated to protecting its creatures.
