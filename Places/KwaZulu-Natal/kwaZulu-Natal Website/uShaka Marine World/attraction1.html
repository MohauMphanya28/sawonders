uShaka Marine World

134 Reviews

Location: 1 King Shaka Ave, Point, Durban, 4001, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa

Top Ocean-Themed Entertainment Destination

uShaka Marine World is a premier marine theme park in Durban, offering fun, education, and adventure for the whole family. From aquariums and dolphin shows to water slides and restaurants, it's a hub of marine wonder.

Prices

Adult - R157 / day

Child - R125 / day

  • Aquarium Adventures Explore one of the largest aquariums in the Southern Hemisphere featuring sharks, turtles, and colorful reef fish.
  • Wet 'n Wild Waterpark Thrilling slides, lazy rivers, and splash zones perfect for all ages to cool off and have fun.
  • Live Animal Shows Be amazed by the dolphin and seal performances in the state-of-the-art stadiums.
  • Shopping & Dining Enjoy beachside restaurants and shops offering everything from souvenirs to gourmet meals.

Located on Durban's Golden Mile, uShaka Marine World combines family fun with marine conservation. Whether you're seeking a splash in the waterpark or a quiet stroll through an underwater tunnel, it's a destination that blends excitement and education on the edge of the Indian Ocean.
