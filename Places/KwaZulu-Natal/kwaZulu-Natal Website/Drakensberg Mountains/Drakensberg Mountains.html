Location: Royal Natal National Park, Drakensberg, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa

Majestic Mountain Escape

The Drakensberg Mountains offer breathtaking scenery, hiking trails, waterfalls, and rich cultural history. Located in the Royal Natal National Park in KwaZulu-Natal, it's a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Prices

Park Entry - R80 / day

Guided Hike - R200 / person

  • Scenic Hiking Trails Trek through lush valleys and up rocky ridges with panoramic views of KwaZulu-Natal's most iconic peaks.
  • Waterfalls & Rock Pools Discover serene waterfalls and swim in natural rock pools surrounded by dramatic mountain scenery.
  • Ancient San Rock Art Explore caves featuring well-preserved San rock paintings that tell the stories of ancient inhabitants.
  • Lodges & Campsites Choose from cozy lodges, eco-resorts, or mountain campsites for a peaceful stay close to nature.

Stretching over 1,000 kilometers, the Drakensberg is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a haven for hikers, birdwatchers, and photographers. With towering peaks, cultural treasures, and serene escapes, it's a must-visit destination located in the heart of KwaZulu-Natal.
