Location: Dundee, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa

Historic War Sites and Cultural Heritage

The Battlefields Route is a historical trail in KwaZulu-Natal where visitors can explore sites of famous Anglo-Zulu and Anglo-Boer War battles, including Isandlwana and Rorke’s Drift. The route offers rich insights into South Africa’s military past.

Prices

Guided Tour - R250 / person

Museum Entry - R50 / person

  • Historical Battle Sites Visit the dramatic battlefields of Isandlwana and Rorke’s Drift where pivotal conflicts shaped history.
  • War Memorials & Museums Explore museums and monuments that preserve the legacy of those who fought in the Zulu, British, and Boer wars.
  • Guided Heritage Tours Learn from expert guides who bring these stories to life with gripping detail and cultural insight.
  • Local Hospitality Enjoy stays at historic lodges and homesteads while dining in traditional South African settings.

The Battlefields Route in KwaZulu-Natal is a unique blend of history and heritage, offering a deep dive into South Africa’s past through preserved sites, memorials, and storytelling. Ideal for history lovers and cultural explorers, this route is both moving and educational.
