Location: West of the Lebombo Mountains on the borders of Mozambique, Limpopo, and Mpumalanga

A wonderful natural destination

Kruger National Park is one of Africa’s largest and most famous game reserves, located in northeastern South Africa. It's celebrated for its rich wildlife, breathtaking scenery, and conservation heritage.

  • Luxury Accommodation Choose from elegant safari lodges and bush camps that provide top-tier service and stunning views of the wild.
  • Wildlife and Safari Experiences Enjoy guided game drives and walking safaris offering close encounters with lions, elephants, rhinos, and more.
  • Nature and Landscapes Explore diverse environments, from savannas to riverine forests, teeming with flora and fauna.
  • Family-Friendly Activities Educational talks, kid-friendly programs, and safe safari adventures for the whole family.

Kruger National Park was established in 1898 and has since become a leading destination for eco-tourism and wildlife conservation. With its vast landscapes and wide array of animals, birds, and plants, it offers visitors a unique and unforgettable experience in the heart of the African wilderness.
