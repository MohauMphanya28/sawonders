Graskop Gorge Lift Co.

117 Reviews

Location: Graskop, Mpumalanga, South Africa

Adventure Meets Nature

Experience the first lift in Africa that takes you down into a gorge! At Graskop Gorge Lift Co., you’ll find adventure activities, forest trails, suspension bridges, and breathtaking views of the Panorama Route.

Prices

Lift: R140 (Adults), R90 (Kids)

Zipline: R270 | Big Swing: R770

  • Lift Into the Gorge Descend 51 meters via the viewing lift into the forest below and walk the interactive trail.
  • Forest Walk Explore walkways, suspension bridges, waterfalls, and native flora in a unique biome.
  • Adrenaline Activities Experience one of the world's highest gorge swings and ziplining across the gorge!
  • Food & Views Relax at the café or restaurant with panoramic views of the gorge and surrounding escarpment.

Graskop Gorge Lift Co. is one of the top eco-tourism and adventure destinations in Mpumalanga. Located on the Panorama Route, this attraction offers a perfect blend of thrill and tranquility, making it ideal for families, tourists, and thrill-seekers alike.
