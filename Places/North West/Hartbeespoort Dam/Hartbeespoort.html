Hartbeespoort Dam

38 Reviews

Location: North West Province, near Pretoria and Johannesburg, South Africa

Relaxation & Adventure by the Water

Hartbeespoort Dam, often called "Harties," is a popular weekend getaway spot known for its beautiful dam, water activities, and surrounding mountains. It’s perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Activities Fees

Boat Cruises - From R200 / person

Cableway Rides - From R250 / adult

  • Scenic Cableway The Harties Cableway offers breathtaking views of the Magaliesberg Mountains and the dam.
  • Water Sports & Cruises Enjoy jet-skiing, paddle boarding, and relaxing boat cruises on the dam.
  • Nature and Wildlife Explore the nearby nature reserves and animal sanctuaries such as the Elephant Sanctuary and Monkey Sanctuary.
  • Restaurants & Markets Local restaurants and markets offer a variety of food and crafts with lakeside views.

Hartbeespoort Dam is a favorite leisure destination located just a short drive from Johannesburg and Pretoria. The area combines the tranquility of a lakeside escape with exciting adventure opportunities like paragliding, hiking, and wildlife interactions. With stunning landscapes, family attractions, and waterside dining, Harties remains a top getaway for both locals and tourists.
