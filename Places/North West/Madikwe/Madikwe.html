Location: North West Province, near the Botswana border, South Africa

Big 5 Safari Destination

Madikwe Game Reserve is one of South Africa’s largest game reserves, known for its Big 5 safaris and malaria-free status. It offers breathtaking views, luxury lodges, and unforgettable wildlife experiences.

  • Big 5 Wildlife Viewing Spot elephants, lions, rhinos, leopards, and buffalo in their natural habitat.
  • Luxury Safari Lodges Stay at premium lodges that blend comfort with wild bush experiences.
  • Malaria-Free Zone Enjoy safaris in a safe, malaria-free environment – ideal for families.
  • Conservation Efforts The reserve is renowned for its eco-tourism and conservation projects.

Madikwe Game Reserve is a 75,000-hectare conservation area located on the border with Botswana. It’s home to more than 60 species of mammals and 300 species of birds. The reserve stands out for its exclusivity, luxurious stays, and commitment to eco-tourism, making it one of South Africa’s top safari destinations.
