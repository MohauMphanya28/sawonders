Magaliesberg Mountains

Location: North West Province, South Africa

Ancient Mountain Range

The Magaliesberg Mountains are among the oldest mountain ranges in the world, estimated to be over 2 billion years old. They offer a unique blend of natural beauty, biodiversity, and adventure.

Entry Fees

Adults - R70 / entry

Children - R40 / entry

  • Hiking & Trails Explore numerous scenic trails that cater to all levels of hikers, from beginners to pros.
  • Adventure Activities Experience zip-lining, rock climbing, hot air ballooning, and mountain biking.
  • Biodiversity Home to various bird species, wildlife, and endemic plant life unique to the region.
  • Charming Stays Enjoy rustic lodges, farm cottages, and eco-retreats nestled in natural surroundings.

The Magaliesberg Mountains stretch across the North West and Gauteng provinces and offer a tranquil escape just a short drive from Johannesburg or Pretoria. Known for their rich geological history, outdoor adventures, and charming accommodation options, the range is a favorite for nature lovers, couples, and families. The area's ancient rock formations and panoramic views make it an ideal destination for exploration and relaxation.
