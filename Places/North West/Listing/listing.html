Recommended Places To Visit in North West

Birdwatcher's Paradise

Barberspan Bird Sanctuary

Barberspan Bird Sanctuary is one of South Africa's most important waterfowl reserves, attracting over 350 bird species. It's ideal for birdwatching, photography, and peaceful nature experiences.

Prices

Adult - R50 / day

Child - R30 / day

Rustic Charm and Heritage

Groot Marico

Groot Marico is a quaint town known for its literary heritage, stunning nature, and traditional South African experiences. It offers visitors a mix of cultural encounters, river activities, and rustic tranquility.

Prices

Adult - R150/ day

Child- R50/ day

Relaxation & Adventure by the Water

Hartbeespoort Dam

Hartbeespoort Dam, often called "Harties," is a popular weekend getaway spot known for its beautiful dam, water activities, and surrounding mountains. It’s perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Prices

Boat Cruises - From R200 / person

Cableway Rides - From R250 / adult

Nature at Its Best

Kgaswane Mountain Reserve

Kgaswane Mountain Reserve offers breathtaking mountain scenery, hiking trails, and abundant wildlife. It’s the perfect place for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore the natural beauty of the North West Province.

Prices

Adults - R60 / day

Children - R35 / day

Big 5 Safari Destination

Madikwe Game Reserve

Madikwe Game Reserve is one of South Africa’s largest game reserves, known for its Big 5 safaris and malaria-free status. It offers breathtaking views, luxury lodges, and unforgettable wildlife experiences.

Prices

Day Visitor Fee - R300 / adult

Guided Game Drive - From R600 / person

A luxury resort and casino

Sun City Resort

Sun City is a luxury resort and casino located in the North West Province of South Africa, near the city of Rustenburg. It's known for its hotels, casinos, golf courses, water parks, and entertainment venues.

Prices

Adult-R445/ day

Child - R306 / day
