Barberspan Bird Sanctuary

29 Reviews

Location: North West Province, near Delareyville, South Africa

Birdwatcher's Paradise

Barberspan Bird Sanctuary is one of South Africa's most important waterfowl reserves, attracting over 350 bird species. It's ideal for birdwatching, photography, and peaceful nature experiences.

Entry Fees

Adult - R50 / day

Child - R30 / day

  • Exceptional Birding Spot pelicans, flamingos, ducks, and other water birds throughout the year.
  • Wetland Habitat A large freshwater lake and reedbeds provide shelter for birds and aquatic life.
  • Photography Hotspot The calm waters and diverse species make it a dream location for nature photographers.
  • Relaxing Trails Take a walk along marked paths and enjoy the tranquil surroundings.

Located in the North West Province, Barberspan Bird Sanctuary is a designated Ramsar wetland site and part of the Barberspan Nature Reserve. It's a vital breeding and migratory site for birds, especially during the wet season. A must-visit destination for eco-tourism and anyone passionate about birdlife.
