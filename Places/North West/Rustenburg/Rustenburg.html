Rustenburg Nature Reserve

38 Reviews

Location: Rustenburg, North West Province, South Africa

Scenic Nature Reserve

The Rustenburg Nature Reserve offers a peaceful escape into nature with its beautiful landscapes, diverse wildlife, and outdoor adventure activities. Ideal for families, birdwatchers, and nature lovers.

Entry Fees

Adults - R60 / entry

Children - R30 / entry

  • Game Viewing See antelope, giraffe, and other wildlife in their natural environment.
  • Hiking Trails Several marked trails for scenic hikes and nature walks.
  • Bird Watching A haven for bird enthusiasts with over 250 recorded species.
  • Accommodation Self-catering cottages and camping sites are available within the reserve.

Located at the foot of the Magaliesberg mountain range, Rustenburg Nature Reserve is a hidden gem for those seeking a relaxing retreat in nature. With its unique blend of highveld vegetation, rich biodiversity, and panoramic views, it’s an excellent destination for weekend getaways, school outings, and eco-tourism activities.
