Kgaswane Mountain Reserve

33 Reviews

Location: Near Rustenburg, North West Province, South Africa

Nature at Its Best

Kgaswane Mountain Reserve offers breathtaking mountain scenery, hiking trails, and abundant wildlife. It’s the perfect place for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore the natural beauty of the North West Province.

Entry Fees

Adult - R60 / day

Child - R35 / day

  • Scenic Hiking Trails Multiple trails including the Peglarae and Summit trails offer spectacular views and varying difficulty levels.
  • Birdwatching Over 320 bird species can be spotted, making it a paradise for birdwatchers.
  • Wildlife Viewing Home to antelope, baboons, and other mammals, the reserve is a natural haven.
  • Camping & Lodging Camping facilities and mountain chalets are available for overnight stays.

Nestled in the Magaliesberg mountain range, Kgaswane Mountain Reserve is a natural retreat just outside Rustenburg. It provides a peaceful environment rich in biodiversity, with great opportunities for hiking, photography, and relaxation. Whether you're seeking adventure or serenity, this reserve has something to offer.
