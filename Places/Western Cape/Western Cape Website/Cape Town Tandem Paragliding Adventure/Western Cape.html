Tandem paragliding in Cape Town has grown into one of the city’s most thrilling adventure activities, offering breathtaking views of Table Mountain, the Atlantic Ocean, and the Twelve Apostles.Paragliding as a sport emerged globally in the 1980s, and Cape Town’s ideal wind conditions and mountainous terrain made it a natural hub.Signal Hill and Lion’s Head became key launch sites due to their reliable south-easterly winds (known as the "Cape Doctor").Early enthusiasts were mostly solo pilots, but by the late 1990s, tandem flights for tourists began gaining popularity. .
