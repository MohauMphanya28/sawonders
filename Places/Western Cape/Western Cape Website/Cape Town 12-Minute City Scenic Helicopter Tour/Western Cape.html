City in the Clouds: 12 Minutes of Pure Luxury

Take a scenic 12-minute helicopter flight along the coast of Cape Town, taking in panoramic views of the stadium, beautiful neighborhoods, and iconic natural landmarks.

  • Table Mountain & Lion’s Head Soar above one of the New7Wonders of Nature.
  • Robben Island Glimpse the historic island where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned.
  • V&A Waterfront Fly over Cape Town’s bustling harbor.
  • Camps Bay & Twelve Apostles Stunning coastal views with the Atlantic Ocean

The 12-Minute City Scenic Helicopter Tour in Cape Town is one of the most popular short helicopter experiences, offering breathtaking aerial views of the city’s iconic landmarks.Helicopter tourism in Cape Town began gaining popularity in the late 20th century, as advancements in aviation technology made scenic flights more accessible.The 12-minute tour emerged as an ideal option for tourists seeking a quick but spectacular overview of Cape Town’s beauty without a long flight commitment.Companies like Helicopters, Cape Town Helicopters, and others began offering this tour, capitalizing on Cape Town’s dramatic coastline and mountainscapes.
