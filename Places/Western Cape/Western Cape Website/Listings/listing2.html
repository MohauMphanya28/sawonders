Recommended Places To Visit in North West

Ride the Dunes in Style – WILDX Atlantis Quad Adventure + Breathtaking Photo Stops!

WILDX Atlantis Quad Dune Adventure and Photo Stop

Embark on an exhilarating quad bike ride across Cape Town's only real white sand dunes at Atlantis Dunes. Join your guide on an exciting excursion and take in panoramic views from a scenic viewpoint.

Prices

Quad Bike Adventure (1-hour tour): - R1,100 per person

Extended Tours (2 hours or more):,400 per person

Photo Stop: Often included in the tour, but some operators may charge a small fee (R50 - R100) if it's a dedicated stop.

Helmet & Gear: Usually included in the price.

Passenger Rides (if available): - R500 (some tours don’t allow passengers).

Wine, Views & Trolley Rides – The Ultimate Franschhoek Escape

Franschhoek Wine Tram Hop-on Hop-off

The Franschhoek Wine Tram Hop-on Hop-off is a popular tourist experience in the Franschhoek Valley, near Cape Town, South Africa. It offers a unique way to explore the region's world-renowned wineries by combining a scenic tram and open-air bus ride with wine tastings, vineyard tours, and gourmet dining.Book your scenic journey from Cape Town to the Franschhoek Wine Tram. Visit various wine farms and enjoy optional wine tastings, gourmet cuisine, and more on this adventure through the Cape Winelands.About this activity.

Prices

Adult Ticket:
Standard Line:R 290 per person
Premium Wineries Line: R 400 per person

Child Ticket (6–17 years):
R 150 (some lines may not allow children)

Infants (0–5 years):
Free (must sit on a parent’s lap)

Champagne Skies & Cape Wines – A Dream Day Out

Hot air Ballon and Cape Wine lands Private guided tour

Defy gravity and hover over the Stellenbosch winelands in a hot air balloon, Explore the historic towns of Stellenbosch and Franschhoek and experience South Africa's award-winning wines.

Prices

Hot Air Balloon Rides (Private)
Shared Flight: R2,500 – R3,500 per person (group experience).
Private Exclusive Flight (for 2-4 people):R12,000 (entire basket, sunrise flight, champagne breakfast included).
Larger Groups (6-12 pax):R25,000 (depending on operator).

Cape Winelands Private Guided Tours
Half-Day Tour (4-5 hrs):R3,500 – R6,000 per group (2-4 people).
Full-Day Tour (8-9 hrs):R6,000 per group (includes transport, guide, and some tastings).
Luxury VIP Tours: R15,000 (private driver, premium estates, gourmet lunch included).
Wine Tasting Fees:Extra (R100 per person per estate).

Step into History: Robben Island Ferry & Prison Experience

Robben Island Ferry Ticket and Guided Prison Tour

Get skip-the-line entry to the Robben Island Ferry, then join a former inmate to explore the prison where South Africa's most influential freedom fighter, Nelson Mandela, was imprisoned for 27 years.

Prices

Adults (International Visitors):R 600

South African Adults (with ID):R 400

Children (Ages 2–18, International):R 300

South African Children (with ID):R 200

Infants (Under 2): Free

Prices

Includes Game drives, meals, sometimes spa access

Garden Route Safari Options (Longer Drive)

Indulge in the Flavors of Stellenbosch

Stellenbosch: Half-Day Wine Tour

Combine wine tastings, cheese tastings, and a cellar visit on this action-packed half-day tour of Stellenbosch. Experience some exciting wine pairings while learning about the wine production.

Prices

Shared Group Tour (small group):
R 800 per person
Includes visits to 2–3 wine estates, tastings, and round-trip transport from Stellenbosch or Cape Town.

Private Tour (exclusive for your group):
R 1,500 per person (or a flat rate of R 3,500 for a private group)
More flexibility, VIP tastings, and possibly lunch.

Self-Drive & Pay-as-You-Go:
Tastings cost R 200 per winery, plus your own transport.

Premium Wine & Food Pairing Tours:
R 2,000 per person (includes gourmet food pairings at top estates).
