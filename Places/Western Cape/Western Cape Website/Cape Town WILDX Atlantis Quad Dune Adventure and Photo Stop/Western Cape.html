The WILDX Atlantis Quad Dune Adventure is an exciting off-road experience in the Atlantis Dunes, located about 40 km north of Cape Town, South Africa. This adventure offers visitors the chance to explore the vast sand dunes on quad bikes (ATVs), with options for guided tours and photo stops to capture the stunning desert-like landscape.The Atlantis Dunes (also known as the Witzand Dunes) are a unique natural sand dune system formed over thousands of years by wind and geological activity.The area was once part of a larger coastal dune field, but due to urbanization and mining, only a portion remains as a conservation-worthy site.In the 1970s, the dunes were used for military training exercises by the South African Defence Force.Today, the dunes are a protected area but allow controlled recreational activities like quad biking, sandboarding, and 4x4 off-roading.
