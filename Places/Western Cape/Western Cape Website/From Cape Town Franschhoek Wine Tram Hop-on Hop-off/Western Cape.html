The Franschhoek Wine Tram Hop-on Hop-off is a popular tourist experience in the Franschhoek Valley, near Cape Town, South Africa. It offers a unique way to explore the region's world-renowned wineries by combining a scenic tram and open-air bus ride with wine tastings, vineyard tours, and gourmet dining.Book your scenic journey from Cape Town to the Franschhoek Wine Tram. The Franschhoek Wine Tram Hop-on Hop-off experience was launched to showcase the Franschhoek Valley’s rich winemaking heritage, dating back to the late 17th century when French Huguenot settlers planted the region’s first vineyards. Modeled after traditional tram systems, this unique tour began operating in 2012, combining vintage-style trams and open-air buses to connect visitors with world-class wine estates. The hop-on, hop-off concept allows travelers to explore the valley’s scenic landscapes, historic Cape Dutch farms, and award-winning wineries at their own pace. Today, it remains one of South Africa’s most popular wine tourism attractions, blending history, luxury, and the region’s renowned hospitality.
