Rhythmic & Flavorful Experiences

The African Dinner and Drumming Experience with Transfer is a popular cultural tour that offers visitors an immersive evening of traditional African cuisine, music, and entertainment.

Prices

R 900 per person (including transfers).

  • Traditional African Dinner Enjoy a delicious buffet-style meal featuring local dishes such as bobotie (spiced minced meat), potjiekos (stew), pap (maize porridge), and other South African specialties.Vegetarian options are usually available.
  • Live Drumming Session Participate in an interactive drumming circle where you’ll learn to play traditional African rhythms under the guidance of skilled performers.The experience often includes other forms of live music and dance performances.
  • Cultural Performances Enjoy vibrant displays of African dance, storytelling, and singing, showcasing the rich heritage of South Africa and the continent.
  • Comfortable Transfers The experience typically includes round-trip transportation from select Cape Town hotels, making it convenient for tourists.
