Port Elizabeth Opera House

Step into history and culture at the Port Elizabeth Opera House, Africa’s oldest functioning theatre, established in 1892 and still hosting world-class performances today. Nestled in the heart of Gqeberha’s historic city centre, the Opera House blends timeless elegance with modern creativity. From captivating stage plays and local talent showcases to powerful dance, music, and storytelling, this theatre offers a rich experience for art lovers, history buffs, and curious travelers alike.

  • A Cultural Landmark Enjoy performances in a theatre steeped in history, with Victorian architecture, antique interiors, and a deep legacy of storytelling and artistic expression.
  • Diverse Performances The Opera House hosts a variety of events including theatre plays, traditional Xhosa performances, jazz nights, poetry sessions, and dance shows.
  • Community and Creativity Home to grassroots art movements and national tours, the venue also provides a platform for emerging local talent and cultural workshops.
  • Unique Guided Tours Explore the backstage world with guided tours that reveal the Opera House's hidden passageways, rich history, and architectural charm.

Whether you're drawn by the drama of a live play, the rhythm of local music, or the magic of historic architecture, the Port Elizabeth Opera House is a must-visit destination that captures the spirit of South African arts and heritage. Take a seat, soak in the ambiance, and let the stories come to life.
