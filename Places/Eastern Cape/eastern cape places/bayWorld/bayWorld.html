The Marine life

Bayworld is a family-friendly museum and oceanarium complex located on the beachfront in Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth), South Africa. It features a blend of marine life displays, historical exhibits, and cultural heritage collections. Originally opened in the 1950s, Bayworld has been a longtime favorite for both locals and tourists. Visitors can explore everything from dinosaur fossils and shipwrecks to live reptiles and marine animals. Though the dolphinarium has closed, the complex still offers engaging displays and a rich learning experience for children and adults alike. Located near popular beachfront attractions, Bayworld is perfect for educational outings and seaside exploration.
