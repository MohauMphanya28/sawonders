Bloukrans Bungy Jump

Dare to defy gravity at Bloukrans Bridge the world’s highest commercial bridge bungy jump at a heart racing 216 meters above the Bloukrans River Gorge. Operated by Face Adrenalin, this iconic adventure is nestled in the stunning Tsitsikamma region along South Africa’s Garden Route. Whether you’re a seasoned thrill-seeker or a first-time jumper, Bloukrans delivers an unforgettable rush with unmatched views and world class safety. Walk the suspended catwalk, take in the roaring river below, then leap into the experience of a lifetime.

Prices

Adult - R1400 / Jump

Spectator - R150

  • Extreme Thrill & Safety Feel the adrenaline with a 216-meter drop while under the care of expert staff and top-notch safety systems certified to international standards.
  • Breathtaking Scenery Take in panoramic views of the lush Tsitsikamma forest and deep gorge as you stand on one of Africa’s most famous bridges.
  • Memorable Experience Capture your moment with professional video and photo packages to relive your jump and share the experience.
  • Accessible for All Open to jumpers aged 14 and up (with parental consent if under 18). Spectators can also walk the catwalk and enjoy the vibe.

Bloukrans Bungy is more than just a jump it’s a life-changing moment of courage, freedom, and exhilaration. Whether you're crossing it off your bucket list or seeking a thrill like no other, this experience will leave your heart racing and your spirit soaring.
