Recommended Places To Visit in Eastern cape

The Animals National park

Addo Elephant National Park

Addo Elephant National Park, a wildlife sanctuary offering an unforgettable safari experience. Home to the renowned Big 5 and one of the densest elephant populations in Africa, Addo boasts unrivaled biodiversity and a malaria-free environment.

Prices

Adult - R360 / day

The Marine life

Bayworld

Bayworld is a family-friendly museum and oceanarium complex located on the beachfront in Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth), South Africa. It features a blend of marine life displays, historical exhibits, and cultural heritage collections. Originally opened in the 1950s, Bayworld has been a longtime favorite for both locals and tourists.

Prices

Adult - R40 / day

Child - R30 / day

Bloukrans Bungy Jump

Bloukrans Bungy

Dare to defy gravity at Bloukrans Bridge the world’s highest commercial bridge bungy jump at a heart racing 216 meters above the Bloukrans River Gorge.

Prices

Adult - R1400 / Jump

Spectator - R150

Blackwater Tubing Tsitsikamma

Blackwater Tubing

Escape into the wild beauty of the Storms River Gorge with Blackwater Tubing, an unforgettable water adventure in the heart of the Tsitsikamma Forest.

Prices

Adults - R695 / PP

Children (12-17 years) - R550/ PP

Port Elizabeth Opera House

The Guild Theatre

Step into history and culture at the Port Elizabeth Opera House, Africa’s oldest functioning theatre, established in 1892 and still hosting world-class performances today.

Prices

Average range – R100 to R300

Guided theatre tours – R50 per person
