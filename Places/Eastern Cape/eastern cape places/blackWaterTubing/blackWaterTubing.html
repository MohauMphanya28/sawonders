Blackwater Tubing Tsitsikamma

Escape into the wild beauty of the Storms River Gorge with Blackwater Tubing, an unforgettable water adventure in the heart of the Tsitsikamma Forest. Drift along crystal-clear pools, paddle through narrow rock passages, and brave gentle rapids – all while surrounded by towering cliffs and lush indigenous forest. Guided by experienced professionals, this eco-adventure blends peace, excitement, and awe-inspiring nature in one magical experience.

Prices

Adults – R695 / per person

Children (12–17 years) – R550 per person

  • Natural Beauty & Adventure Float through the stunning Storms River Canyon, a hidden paradise filled with emerald pools, rocky cliffs, and ancient trees.
  • Guided Eco-Tour Led by knowledgeable local guides, the experience includes environmental education, cave exploration, and fun commentary.
  • Safe & Family-Friendly All equipment is provided, and guides ensure safety at every step. Suitable for beginners, families, and adventure lovers aged 12 and up.
  • Optional Thrills For the bold, optional rock jumps into the deep waters are available — perfect for a shot of adrenaline and amazing photos.

Blackwater Tubing is more than a water ride it’s a journey through one of South Africa’s most pristine gorges. Whether you’re after calm, scenic floating or thrilling splashes, this Tsitsikamma experience will leave you feeling connected, recharged, and inspired by nature’s wonders.
