Polokwane

47 Reviews

Location: Limpopo Province, South Africa

Capital of Limpopo

Polokwane is a vibrant city offering a mix of culture, wildlife, shopping, and heritage. It’s a hub for business and tourism in the region and a gateway to Limpopo’s many attractions.

Prices

City Tours - R250 / person

Game Reserve Entry - R100 / adult | R60 / child

  • Modern Infrastructure Polokwane features modern buildings, shopping malls, and civic centers catering to locals and tourists alike.
  • Wildlife Experiences Visit the Polokwane Game Reserve or nearby parks to see antelope, zebras, and more in a natural habitat.
  • Cultural Attractions Explore the Polokwane Art Museum, Hugh Exton Photographic Museum, and cultural villages.
  • Dining & Local Cuisine Enjoy a variety of restaurants serving traditional and international dishes across the city.

Polokwane is a lively city nestled in the heart of Limpopo. Whether you're exploring its game reserves, museums, or bustling streets, Polokwane offers something for everyone. It's a dynamic place where modern development meets rich heritage and serves as an ideal base for exploring the rest of the province.
