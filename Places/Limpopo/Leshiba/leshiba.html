Leshiba Wilderness

42 Reviews

Location: Soutpansberg Mountains, Limpopo Province, South Africa

Eco-luxury and cultural experience

Leshiba Wilderness is an award-winning eco-lodge and nature reserve situated high in the Soutpansberg Mountains of Limpopo. It offers a unique combination of wildlife, sustainability, art, and Venda culture.

Prices

Adult - R550 / day

Child - R350 / day

  • Luxury Accommodation Stay in artistically designed Venda-style lodges and eco-houses that blend comfort with local heritage.
  • Nature and Wildlife Home to giraffes, zebras, leopards, and over 250 bird species, Leshiba offers hiking, biking, and guided safaris.
  • Cultural Immersion Explore the local Venda culture through art, architecture, and storytelling woven into the design of the lodges.
  • Family & Wellness Enjoy yoga retreats, tranquil settings, and family-friendly eco activities in untouched natural beauty.

Leshiba Wilderness is not just a nature reserve, but an immersive cultural and ecological experience. Perched atop the Soutpansberg range, it’s a hidden gem offering eco-conscious luxury, access to nature, and meaningful engagement with South Africa’s rich heritage.
