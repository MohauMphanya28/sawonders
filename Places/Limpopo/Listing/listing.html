Recommended Places To Visit in Limpopo

Heart of Venda Culture

Thohoyandou

Thohoyandou is the vibrant capital of the Vhembe District and a cultural hub for the Venda people. It features a mix of traditional and modern attractions including markets, museums, and nearby nature reserves.

Prices

Adult - R100 / day

Child - R60 / day

Natural Wonder of Africa

Blyde Canyon Nature Reserve

Blyde Canyon Nature Reserve is one of the largest canyons in the world and a breathtaking destination known for its lush subtropical vegetation, towering cliffs, and dramatic viewpoints.

Prices

Adult - R100/ day

Child- R60/ day

Cradle of Human History

Makapan’s Valley

Makapan’s Valley is a World Heritage Site recognized for its deep anthropological and archaeological significance. It preserves the story of early humans and their environments from over 3 million years ago.

Prices

Adult - R415 / day

Child - R305 / day

Nature Escape in the Mountains

Magoebaskloof

Magoebaskloof is a scenic mountainous region offering lush indigenous forests, hiking trails, waterfalls, and tranquil retreats. It’s perfect for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and adventure seekers.

Prices

Adults - R80 / day

Children - R40 / day

Botanical and Cultural Gem

Modjadji Cycad Reserve

The Modjadji Cycad Reserve is home to the world’s largest population of the rare Encephalartos transvenosus cycad. It is also culturally significant due to its connection to the Rain Queen Modjadji, a mystical figure in local tradition.

Prices

Adult - R50 / day

Child - R25 / day
