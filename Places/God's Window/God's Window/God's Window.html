God’s Window

94 Reviews

Location: Panorama Route, Mpumalanga, South Africa

Breath-taking Views Over the Lowveld

God's Window is a famous viewpoint on the Drakensberg escarpment, offering panoramic views of lush forested ravines, cliffs, and the vast Lowveld stretching out below. On clear days, you can even glimpse the Kruger National Park and Mozambique.

Prices

Adult - R30 / entry

Child - R20 / entry

  • Panoramic Views Unmatched views over cliffs and forests with a dramatic drop of over 700 meters.
  • Walking Trails Enjoy forested trails and lookout spots perfect for nature lovers and hikers.
  • Photography Spot A must-stop for stunning landscape photos, especially at sunrise or misty dawns.
  • Rainforest Reserve Explore the tropical rainforest section at the top, filled with ferns and mosses.

God’s Window is part of the Panorama Route in Mpumalanga, South Africa. It’s one of the region’s most iconic viewpoints, named for the heavenly views it provides. The area combines cliffs, forest, mist, and silence — creating a spiritual and awe-inspiring experience. Perfect for travelers exploring the natural beauty of South Africa.
