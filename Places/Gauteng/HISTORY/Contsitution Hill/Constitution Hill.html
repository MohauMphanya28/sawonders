Contsitution Hill

11 Kotze Street, Braamfontein, Johannesburg, South Africa

Getting There

Constitution Hill is accessible via car or taxi. Secure underground parking is available for visitors at a nominal fee.

  • What To Expect Explore the Old Fort, Number Four Prison, and the Women's Jail, where activists like Nelson Mandela and Mahatma Gandhi were once detained. The Constitutional Court is open to the public, featuring a collection of over 200 South African artworks

Constitution Hill in Johannesburg is a profound testament to South Africa's journey from oppression to democracy. Once a notorious prison complex, it now houses the Constitutional Court, symbolizing justice and human rights.​
