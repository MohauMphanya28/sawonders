Sun City is a luxury resort and casino located in the North West Province of South Africa, near the city of Rustenburg. It's known for its hotels, casinos, golf courses, water parks, and entertainment venues. Opened in 1979, Sun City became famous for its lavish attractions and for hosting major concerts and sporting events. It's often described as a "mini Las Vegas" and is close to Pilanesberg National Park, making it popular for combining luxury with safari experiences.
