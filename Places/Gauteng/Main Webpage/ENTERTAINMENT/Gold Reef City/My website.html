  • Family-Friendly Activities Family and Kids Rides: With 7 family rides and 21 dedicated kids rides, there's fun for everyone.

Gold Reef City is a premier entertainment destination located in Johannesburg, South Africa, built on the site of a former gold mine that closed in 1971. The park is themed around the gold rush that started in 1886 on the Witwatersrand, and the buildings are designed to mimic this period.
