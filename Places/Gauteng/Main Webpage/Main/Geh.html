Welcome to Gauteng Province

Discover South Africa's vibrant heart a blend of modern cities, breathtaking nature, and rich history. Gauteng invites you to explore its many attractions, from thrilling entertainment to serene landscapes and important historical sites.

Entertainment

Gold Reef City

Theme park and casino built around an old gold mine, offering rides, shows, and historical tours.

Montecasino

Italian-themed complex with restaurants, theaters, shopping, and nightlife experiences.

Nelson Mandela Square

Stylish Sandton hub with shopping, fine dining, and the iconic Mandela statue.

Maboneng Precinct

Trendy arts district filled with galleries, restaurants, rooftop bars, and local shops.

Nature

Walter Sisulu Botanical Garden

Beautiful gardens featuring waterfalls, picnic spots, and diverse birdlife near Johannesburg.

Rietvlei Nature Reserve

Spot rhinos, buffalo, and other wildlife in this peaceful reserve near Pretoria.

Johannesburg Zoo

One of South Africa's largest zoos, perfect for family outings and educational tours.

Cradle of Humankind

UNESCO World Heritage Site with ancient caves and exhibits about human origins.

History

Apartheid Museum

Powerful museum documenting South Africa's journey from apartheid to democracy.

Constitution Hill

Historic site and museum showcasing South Africa's human rights history.

Union Buildings

Government seat in Pretoria with stunning gardens and panoramic city views.

Hector Pieterson Memorial

Memorial honoring the students of the 1976 Soweto Uprising.
