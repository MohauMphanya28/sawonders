Recommended Places To Visit in North West

A Luxury Resort and Casino

Sun City Resort

Sun City is a luxury resort and casino located in the North West Province of South Africa, near the city of Rustenburg. It's known for its hotels, casinos, golf courses, water parks, and entertainment venues.

Prices

Adult - R415 / day

Child - R305 / day

Aerial Cableway

Aerial Cableway Hartbeespoort

The Hartbeespoort Aerial Cableway is South Africa's longest mono-cableway and a premier attraction in the North West Province. It offers a scenic 1.2 km ride with panoramic views of the Hartbeespoort Dam and Magaliesberg Mountains.

Prices

All ages - R360 / day

Water park located within the Sun City Resort

Valley of Waves

The Valley of Waves is a premier water park located within the Sun City Resort in South Africa's North West province. Designed to resemble a tropical paradise, it features palm-fringed beaches, lush vegetation, and a variety of water attractions suitable for all ages.

Prices

Adult - R415 / day

Child - R305 / day

Wildlife reserve

Pilanesberg National Park

Ready for the ultimate safari adventure? Discover Pilanesberg National Park — where ancient volcanic beauty meets thrilling Big Five encounters, all just a short drive from the city.

Prices

Children - R30 / day

Game Reserve

Madikwe Game Reserve

Venture into the wild heart of Madikwe Game Reserve — track the Big Five, explore rugged landscapes, and answer the call of true African adventure.
