Montecasino

1 Montecasino Blvd, Fourways, Sandton, 2021, South Africa

Teatro: One of South Africa's largest theatres, seating up to 1,870 guests. It hosts major productions, including musicals like The Lion King.

  • Accommodation The Palazzo Montecasino: A luxury hotel with 246 rooms and suites, offering top-tier amenities.
  • Casino and Gaming Montecasino's casino operates 24/7, featuring over 1,700 slot machines and 70 gaming tables, including games like roulette, blackjack, and poker.
  • Dining and Nightlife With over 40 restaurants, cafes, and bars, Montecasino offers a diverse culinary experience, from fine dining to casual eateries.
  • Family-Friendly Activities Family Fun: Includes ten-pin bowling, laser tag, arcade games at The Magic Company, and the Bambini Crèche for younger children.

Montecasino is a premier entertainment complex located in Fourways, Johannesburg, South Africa. Designed to replicate an ancient Tuscan village, it offers a unique blend of leisure, gaming, dining, and family-friendly activities.
