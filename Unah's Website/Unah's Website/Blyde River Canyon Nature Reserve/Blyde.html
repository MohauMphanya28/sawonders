Blyde Canyon Nature Reserve

45 Reviews

Location: Mpumalanga and Limpopo border, South Africa

Natural Wonder of Africa

Blyde Canyon Nature Reserve is one of the largest canyons in the world and a breathtaking destination known for its lush subtropical vegetation, towering cliffs, and dramatic viewpoints.

Prices

Adult - R100 / day

Child - R60 / day

  • Scenic Viewpoints Visit iconic sites like God’s Window, Bourke’s Luck Potholes, and the Three Rondavels.
  • Outdoor Adventures Enjoy hiking, boat cruises on the Blyde Dam, and nature trails through beautiful landscapes.
  • Biodiversity Home to diverse plant and animal life, including rare bird species and stunning flora.
  • Photography Paradise The canyon offers some of the most photogenic scenery in all of South Africa.

The Blyde Canyon Nature Reserve is a must-visit for nature lovers and photographers. As one of the greenest and deepest canyons in the world, it offers unforgettable views, unique biodiversity, and a range of outdoor activities that highlight the majesty of South Africa’s natural heritage.
