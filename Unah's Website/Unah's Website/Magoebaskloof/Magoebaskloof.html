Magoebaskloof

Location: Between Polokwane and Tzaneen, Limpopo Province, South Africa

Nature Escape in the Mountains

Magoebaskloof is a scenic mountainous region offering lush indigenous forests, hiking trails, waterfalls, and tranquil retreats. It’s perfect for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and adventure seekers.

Prices

Adult - R80 / day

Child - R40 / day

  • Forests and Waterfalls Home to the Woodbush Forest Reserve and cascading waterfalls like Debengeni Falls.
  • Hiking and Trails Several well-marked hiking routes through dense forest and mountain paths.
  • Adventure Activities Try canopy tours, mountain biking, and trout fishing in scenic surroundings.
  • Scenic Beauty Mist-covered valleys, diverse birdlife, and breathtaking panoramic views.

Magoebaskloof is a peaceful retreat in the heart of Limpopo. With its misty mountains, deep gorges, and lush forest landscapes, it’s a sanctuary for those looking to relax in nature or embark on eco-adventures. From zip-lining across canyons to exploring waterfalls, the area offers an unforgettable experience for all ages.
